Building a cross-brand design system

Period Covered: Oct 2024 - 2026
Client: Travel & Leisure
Role: Key Designer

This project provides creative direction for digital technology initiatives, developing a flexible platform to support brand sites for a portfolio of nearly 20 resort, club, and lifestyle brands in Vacation Ownership.

Challenges

The project required building a flexible, white-labeled design system supporting up to 20 unique brands with different visual identities, while maintaining a cohesive user experience and consistent interaction patterns across all adaptive components. In parallel, we redesigned existing brand sites to align with the new system, balancing brand differentiation with usability best practices and collaborating closely with stakeholders to ensure scalability, design quality, and accessibility across the entire ecosystem.
