Healthcare native ecosystem + Design System

Period Covered: May 2021 - Sep 2022 | Client: Walgreens Boots Alliance | Role: Key Designer

Healthcare native ecosystem

Consumer-centric healthcare ecosystem starting with Caregivers and Seniors by creating a personalized, physical and digital healthcare platform for Chronic Condition management leveraging Walgreens’ reach.

Challenges

Creation of an application for a target audience of 65+ years old has a lot of limitations, including requirements for accessibility, a clear and transparent structure of interaction and automation.

Achievements

The design team has developed a consistent research-based accessible design that covers all customer and user needs. We managed to expand the platform and attract new sub-branded customers.

Most of the design solutions on the project are tested in collaboration with the research team. Since the beginning of the project, more than 25 studies have been carried out to create a user-oriented application. Were held scenario-based surveys, competitive audit, resonance testing, A/B preference, likert scale, task-based study, card sorting, and heuristic review. This approach made it possible to create an application focused on the consumer and helping him to solve the problems of taking care of his health on a daily basis.

Design System and Style Guide

At the start, due to strict deadlines and unsettled processes, the design was developed by individual designers who were not connected as a team. So the design for the MVP was inconsistent, which caused problems for both designers and developers.

Achievements

We managed to transfer the design project to a completely new software platform and convince the customer of the need for a unified and systematic approach. A design library and style guide were created to facilitate collaboration between designers. Subsequently, design tokens were introduced into the system and there was established a close collaboration with developers. All components are reviewed by the accessibility testing team to increase the application quality.
